# Focusd Demo Script (2 Minutes)

## Plan
- **Duration**: 2 minutes maximum
- **Goal**: Professional, shareable demo showcasing features and VideoDB integration
- **Structure**: Quick intro → feature walkthrough → VideoDB power explanation
- **No installation steps** - jump straight into the running app
- **Focus**: Real productivity insights powered by real-time video intelligence

---

## Script

### [0:00-0:15] Hook & Introduction
**SHOW**: Focusd app icon → Launch app → Dashboard view

> "Meet Focusd - your AI productivity partner that actually understands what you're doing. It watches your screen, tracks your work in real-time, and gives you insights you can act on. Let's see how it works."

---

### [0:15-0:45] Live Activity Timeline
**SHOW**: Navigate to Timeline view, show live updates happening

> "This is your live activity timeline. Every few seconds, Focusd captures what's on your screen and understands the context - which app you're in, what you're working on, even which project you're contributing to.
>
> Notice how it's updating in real-time as I switch between VS Code, Chrome, and Slack. It's not just tracking apps - it's understanding the content."

**ACTION**: Switch between 2-3 apps to show live tracking

---

### [0:45-1:10] AI Summaries & Drill Down
**SHOW**: Click on a session summary card, then show drill-down view

> "Here's where it gets powerful. Focusd automatically generates AI summaries of your work sessions.
>
> Click any time block, and you get a detailed breakdown: exact app usage, project time distribution, and a natural language summary of what you accomplished.
>
> No manual time logging. No guessing. Just accurate insights."

**ACTION**: Hover over/click a summary card, show the drill-down modal with charts

---

### [1:10-1:35] Dashboard & Daily Recap
**SHOW**: Navigate to Dashboard, then Reports tab

> "The dashboard gives you the big picture: total tracked time, productive vs. idle time, and your top applications.
>
> And at the end of each day, Focusd generates a complete recap with highlights and actionable suggestions to improve your focus tomorrow."

**ACTION**: Scroll through dashboard charts, click to Daily Recap report

---

### [1:35-2:00] VideoDB Power (The Secret Sauce)
**SHOW**: Quick visual of: screen capture → VideoDB logo → AI summary output

> "So how does this work? Focusd is built on VideoDB's capture SDK.
>
> VideoDB continuously records your screen, indexes it in real-time using vision AI, and extracts structured data - app names, visible text, context - every few seconds.
>
> This indexed video stream feeds a 5-layer summarization pipeline that turns raw screen captures into actionable productivity intelligence.
>
> That's the power of VideoDB: turning video into searchable, queryable, intelligent data."

**SHOW**: End on Focusd logo + "Built with VideoDB" tagline

---

## Filming Notes

1. **Pre-record with realistic data** - have actual work sessions tracked before demo
2. **Smooth transitions** - use keyboard shortcuts to navigate quickly
3. **Show live updates** - critical to demonstrate real-time capability
4. **Keep cursor movement purposeful** - no random hovering
5. **Background music**: subtle, energetic, professional
6. **Voiceover**: confident, conversational pace
7. **Text overlays** (optional):
   - "Real-time Activity Tracking" (Timeline)
   - "AI-Generated Insights" (Summaries)
   - "Powered by VideoDB" (final frame)

## Technical Setup

- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or 1280x720
- **Screen recording**: Use OBS or ScreenFlow (not Focusd itself to avoid recursion!)
- **Audio**: Clear voiceover, minimal background noise
- **Export**: MP4, H.264, 30fps

## Key Messaging

✅ **DO emphasize**:
- Real-time understanding (not just app tracking)
- AI-generated insights (not manual logging)
- VideoDB's vision AI indexing capability
- Actionable productivity data

❌ **DON'T mention**:
- Installation steps
- Technical setup
- API keys or configuration
- Pricing or credits
